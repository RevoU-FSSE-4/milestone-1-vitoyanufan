Lets Take A Look To Our Gallery

Our park is home to over a thousand amazing animals and is a sanctuary for some of the world's rare and endangered species. Witness yourself with rare & endangered species like the Lemur, Lion, Elephant, Girrafe, Hippos and many more.

Lemur
They are endemic to the island of Madagascar. Most existing lemurs are small, have a pointed snout, large eyes, and a long tail.

Elephant
the Largest land mammals on earth and have distinctly massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks.

Lion
Often described as the king of the jungle, lions are a distinctive and well-known big cat species.

Girrafe
the Tallest of all land animals. They are able to browse foliage almost six metres from the ground.

Hippopotamus
Amphibious African ungulate mammal. Often considered to be the second largest land animal (after the elephant)

Zebra
Species of wild horse and are native to the African continent. Famous for their black and white stripes.

Orang Utan
Asia's only great ape, As one of our closest living relatives, the orangutan is a highly intelligent, sentient being.
